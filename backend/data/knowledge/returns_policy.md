# Returns Policy

## Return window
Most items may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be
unused, in their original condition, and in the original packaging with tags attached.
Final-sale and clearance items are not returnable.

## How to start a return
Go to Orders, select the order, and choose "Start a return", or ask our support chat to
help you start one. You will receive a prepaid return shipping label by email. Drop the
package at any carrier location within 14 days of creating the return.

## Damaged or defective items
If an item arrives damaged or defective, contact support within 7 days of delivery with a
photo. We will ship a replacement at no cost or issue a full refund including original
shipping charges.

## Exchanges
We do not process direct exchanges. Return the original item for a refund and place a new
order for the replacement size or color.
