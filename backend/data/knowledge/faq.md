# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I track my order?
Open the Orders page or ask the support chat "where is my order?". Every shipped order
has a tracking number and a status (pending, processing, shipped, delivered).

## Can I cancel or change my order?
You can cancel an order any time before it ships, from the Orders page. Once an order has
shipped it cannot be changed — wait for it to arrive and then start a return.

## What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal.

## How long do refunds take?
After we receive your return, refunds post to your original payment method within 5-7
business days. See the Refund Policy for details.

## Do you ship internationally?
Not yet — we currently ship within the United States only.

## How do I contact a human?
Ask the chat to "talk to a human" and we will pass your conversation to a support agent
during business hours (Mon-Fri, 9am-6pm ET).
