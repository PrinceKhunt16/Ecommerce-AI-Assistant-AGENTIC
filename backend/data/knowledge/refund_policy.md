# Refund Policy

## Refund timing
Once we receive and inspect your returned item, refunds are issued to the original
payment method within 5-7 business days. Your bank or card issuer may take a few
additional days to post the credit.

## What is refunded
The full item price is refunded. Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the
return is due to our error or a defective item. Return shipping is free when you use the
prepaid label we provide.

## Refunds for cancelled orders
Orders cancelled before they ship are refunded in full within 3-5 business days. Once an
order has shipped it can no longer be cancelled — start a return after it arrives.

## Partial refunds
Partial refunds may apply to items returned outside the 30-day window, items not in
original condition, or items missing parts or packaging.
