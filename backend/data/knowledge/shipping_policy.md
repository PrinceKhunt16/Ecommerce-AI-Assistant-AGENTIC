# Shipping Policy

## Processing time
Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a shipping confirmation
email with a tracking number once your order leaves our warehouse.

## Shipping options and delivery times
- Standard (free over $50): 3-5 business days.
- Standard (under $50): flat $4.99, 3-5 business days.
- Express: $14.99, 1-2 business days.

## Tracking your order
Track an order anytime from the Orders page or by asking support chat for the status.
Each shipped order has a tracking number; status moves through pending → processing →
shipped → delivered.

## International shipping
We currently ship within the United States only. International shipping is coming soon.

## Delayed or lost packages
If tracking has not updated for 7 days, or a package is marked delivered but you did not
receive it, contact support and we will open an investigation with the carrier.
